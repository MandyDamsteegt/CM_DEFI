Events

Welcome to Karaoke Night!

Event Details

Join us for a fun-filled Karaoke Night where you can showcase your singing talent and have a blast.

Date: Every Friday Night

Time: 8:00 PM - 11:00 PM

Location: The Pub's Karaoke Lounge

Prizes

Prizes will be awarded for the best performances of the night. Sing your heart out and win exciting rewards!

How to Participate

1. Sign up at the registration desk on the night of the event.

2. Choose your favorite songs from our extensive karaoke library.

3. Take the stage and give it your all!

Test Your Knowledge at Pub Quiz Trivia Night!

Event Details

Join us for a night of brain-teasing questions and friendly competition at our Pub Quiz Trivia Night.

Date: Every Wednesday Evening

Time: 7:30 PM - 10:00 PM

Location: The Pub's Trivia Lounge

Prizes

Exciting prizes await the winning teams. Test your knowledge and win great rewards!

How to Participate

1. Form a team with your friends (usually 2-6 people per team).

2. Register your team on the night of the event.

3. Answer trivia questions across various categories to earn points.
